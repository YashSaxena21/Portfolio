About Me

I am a Ph.D. student in Computer Science at the University of Maryland, Baltimore County. My research bridges Neurosymbolic AI and Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) to build trustworthy language agents. I focus on making the generation pipeline transparent and verifiable, developing citation schemes and interpretable architectures that balance coverage with correctness. Beyond core research, I am active in the hackathon community and enjoy mentoring undergraduate students to help turn theoretical ideas into practical tools.
